Government Schemes for Farmers

🌿 Gobardhan (Galvanizing Organic Bio-Agro Resources Dhan)

Supports rural areas in converting cattle dung and other organic waste into compost, biogas, and bio-CNG. Implemented through Swachh Bharat Mission in Karnataka.

Learn More

♻️ Organic & Bio-waste Composting – KVK Dakshina Kannada

Supports farmers in converting crop and farm waste into compost and organic fertilizer through training and demonstrations.

Learn More

♻️ Waste Management in Karnataka (Organic & Agricultural Waste)

An initiative covering organic/compost waste in Karnataka, highlighting compost generation and farm-waste utilisation practices across the state.

Learn More

💧 National Bioenergy Mission (NBEM)

Encourages bioenergy generation from agricultural residues and waste biomass. Active projects are supported across Karnataka.

Learn More

🌱 Karnataka Compost Development Corporation – Farm-Waste Composting Initiative

This initiative by the Karnataka Compost Development Corporation (KCDC) converts agri-waste and organic residues into compost, supporting farmers in Karnataka with low-cost organic fertilizer.

Learn More

🧪 Karnataka State Pollution Control Board – Waste Management Initiatives

KSPCB monitors and supports waste management programs across the state, including organic and farm waste utilization.

Learn More

💡 Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Rural Energy and Development (MGIRED)

Promotes bioenergy projects and rural waste-to-energy systems in Karnataka, focusing on agricultural residues and eco-friendly fuels.

Learn More

🚜 Bengaluru Urban Agriculture Department – Organic Farming Program

Encourages eco-friendly agricultural practices and farm waste composting for farmers in the Bengaluru region.

Learn More

🌻 National Policy for Management of Crop Residues (NPMCR)

A national policy guiding Karnataka’s programs to reduce residue burning and promote composting and reuse of crop waste.
