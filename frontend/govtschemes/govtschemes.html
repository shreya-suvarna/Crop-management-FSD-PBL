Government Schemes for Farmers

🌿 Gobardhan (Galvanizing Organic Bio-Agro Resources Dhan)

Aims to support villages in converting cattle dung and other organic waste into compost, biogas, and bio-CNG for cleaner rural environments.

Learn More

🔥 Crop Residue Management (CRM) Scheme

Encourages farmers to adopt in-situ residue management using Happy Seeders, mulchers, and other machinery to prevent stubble burning.

Learn More

♻️ Waste to Wealth Mission

Part of the Atal Innovation Mission to promote technologies that convert agricultural and other organic waste into useful products and energy.

Learn More

💧 National Bioenergy Mission (NBEM)

Supports production of biogas, bio-CNG, and power generation from agricultural residues, manure, and other organic waste.

Learn More

🌱 Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) – Waste Management Component

Provides financial assistance to states to promote composting units, bio-fertilizers, and organic waste recycling in agriculture.

Learn More

🧪 National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA)

Focuses on sustainable farming, including efficient residue management and compost use to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

Learn More

🌻 National Mission on Oilseeds and Oil Palm (NMOOP)

Promotes utilization of oilseed crop waste for bio-compost and encourages eco-friendly processing to minimize waste.

Learn More

💡 SATAT Initiative (Sustainable Alternative Towards Affordable Transportation)

Launched by the Ministry of Petroleum to set up compressed biogas plants using agricultural waste and biomass residues.

Learn More

🚜 Swachh Bharat Mission – Rural Composting Program

Encourages segregation and composting of biodegradable waste in rural areas, turning farm residues into organic fertilizer.

Learn More
